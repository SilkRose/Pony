# Flying Feelings

## Synopsis:
Fluttershy finds herself feeling different around Rainbow Dash, especially when she is flying. Fluttershy gives into her desires while watching Rainbow fly and joins in, doing her best to fly alongside her and have fun together. After a bit, she starts to do more daring flight maneuvers that bring them closer and closer. Fluttershy notices the blush on Rainbow's face and decides to confess her feelings, she leads Rainbow up into the air before saying I love you, and kissing Dash. As they kiss, Fluttershy grabs dash in, and they free fall together as they kiss.
